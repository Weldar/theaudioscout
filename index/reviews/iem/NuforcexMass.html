Massdrop x Nuforce EDC In-Ear Monitors

A great value pair of IEMs to get you started.

Matt Followell

I purchased these IEMs from Massdrop on June 28 2018 so I have had a decent amount of time to listen to them and get an idea for their sound profile. I have to say for $60 I am quite impressed.

What is in the Box

  • 1 Braided TRS Cable
  • 1 Straight TRRS Cable with in-line Mic
  • 2 Pairs of Large Memory Foam Tips
  • 3 Pairs of Silicon Tips (Sm, Med, Lrg)
  • 1 Shirt Clip
  • 1 Zipper Carrying Case
  • 2 EDC IEMs (thankfully)

Construction

These IEMs are made from a solid plastic that is smooth, sits comfortably in my ears, and yet feel very solid without being weighty.
