Over-Ear Open Back Headphones Reviews
These cans provide little to no sound isolation, which means that not only will you hear the world around you, the world will hear you; however they usually have a wider soundstage and most high end headphones typically fall within this category.

Over-Ear Closed Back Headphones Reviews
As one might guess closed back headphones seal you off from the rest of the world and as an added bonus often have a bit fuller bass for you bass-heads out there. Unfortunately closed back headphones in the more budget categories tend to sound muddy.
